what to do

 
 

activities in Rio de Janeiro and Buzios

christ the redeemor

Visit one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Christ the Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ constructed between 1922 and 1931. At 98 feet high, the statue is located at the peak of the Corcovado mountain in Tijuca Forest National Park and overlooks the city of Rio de Janeiro.

We recommend taking the Corcovado train to access the monument. You can buy tickets here.

the beaches of Rio de Janeiro

You can't visit Rio de Janeiro with out having a beach day at one of the very famous beaches. Visit Copacabana, Ipanema, or our favorite “the Red Beach” which is situated just underneath the sugarloaf mountain.

Be sure to grab a coconut water while you're there, and definitely don't forget your sunscreen.

Sugarloaf Mountain

The sugarloaf mountain has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2012. You can take the historic cable car up that was first constructed in 1912 to the top (just across from the Red Beach) and have panoramic views of the entire city.

We recommend going at the end of the afternoon to get the best sunset view.

 

PONTAL DO ATALAIA- arrial de cabo

Visit the “Caribbean of Brazil” with cristal clear turquoise waters. It's only an 1.5 hour drive from Buzios!

You can access this beach either by boat or taxi. We recommend arriving by taxi which drops you off at the very top of the cliff so you get the best views and then returning by boat. :)

cabo frio

Cabo Frio is only a 45 minute drive from Buzios and has plenty of beautiful beaches.

Visit the “Shell Beach” or “Praia Pero” if you're looking for more natural beaches, or visit “Praia do Forte” for a more lively atmosphere with many different restaurants and bars directly on the beach.

buzios

This picturesque fishing village was originally settled by European pirates and has been elected one of the 10 most beautiful areas in the world. It's famous for its unique combination of rustic charm, architectural harmony, beautiful beaches, natural vegetation and very sophisticated boutiques and restaurants.

Our wedding venue is a short 20 minute drive away.
